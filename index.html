Core Capabilities

Every part of your operation,
accounted for.

Built around how fuel stations actually run in Nigeria — not adapted from a generic platform.

Meter & Sales Reconciliation

Stop the 'Ghost' Litres. Managers can no longer "edit" yesterday’s closing figures to hide a short. ForecourtOS freezes meter readings at shift-change, forcing an instant reconciliation against your bank deposits.

Success: Your paper records and your bank balance finally match to the last Kobo.

01
Expense & Cash Tracking

Audit-Proof Your Cash Flow. Small "miscellaneous" expenses bleed station profits dry. Every Naira spent on-site—from diesel for the gen-set to local levies—is logged and subtracted from sales instantly.

Success: Full visibility on "Net Daily Profit" after all site expenses are settled.

02
Tank & Stock Monitoring

Know Your Wet-Stock Truth. Stop wondering if your underground tanks are leaking or if a delivery was "light." We automatically flag variances between pump sales and physical dips before the shift ends.

Success: Identify delivery shortages and underground leaks in real-time, not at the end of the month.

03
Multi-Station Remote Command

Retire the 9 PM Manager Call. Stop begging for daily reports via WhatsApp. Access a live "Command Centre" that shows total sales, cash-on-hand, and pump status for every station across the country from your phone.

Success: Total operational oversight without setting foot on a forecourt.

04
The System

One system. Every layer of your business.

01
Data Integrity at the Pump

Enforce the Digital Paper Trail.

Attendants log meter readings directly into the OS. Every entry is Password-linked to a specific user and timestamp. Once the shift closes, the data is immutable — impossible to retroactively edit.

So What:

No more "accounting" hidden shortages at 10 PM. What happens at the pump stays at the pump.

02
Real-Time Variance Logic

Instant Leak & Fraud Detection.

The system cross-references pump sales against physical tank dips instantly. If a single liter is unaccounted for, whether through a leak or an unrecorded "side sale", the OS flags it as a Critical Variance before the attendant leaves the site.

So What:

You stop losses while the staff is still on the premises, not three weeks later during an audit.

03
Your Station in Your Pocket

Remote Command, Not Just Monitoring.

Your dashboard isn't just a report; it’s a live pulse. See cash-on-hand, tank levels, and active alerts across your entire fleet in one view on any device, from anywhere.

So What:

No more begging for WhatsApp reports. You gain the freedom to grow because you don't have to be physically present to prevent theft.
