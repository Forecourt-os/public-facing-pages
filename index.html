The System

One system. Every layer of your business.

01

Staff enter. System locks.

Pump attendants log meter readings. Supervisors record tank dips, lodgements, generator usage. Each entry is tied to a shift, a user, and a timestamp — locked after submission.

02

Reconciliation runs automatically.

Cash-at-hand calculates itself. Stock variances surface immediately. Anything outside tolerance is flagged before the day ends — not discovered weeks later.

03

You see everything. From anywhere.

Your executive dashboard shows every station's health in one view. Sales, cash, alerts, report status — on your phone, without calling anyone.
